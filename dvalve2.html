DValve2 is a tap attachment for the urban home.
Littered with sensors and logic cores, DValve2 helps you save water by automatically stopping your tap when the bucket placed underneath gets filled!

DValve2 makes use of solenoid valves and micro-pumps which are driven by a microcontroller with algorithms which minimize the current usage even further.
